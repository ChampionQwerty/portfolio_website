Network+

The CompTIA Network+ certification is a globally recognized, vendor-neutral credential that validates foundational networking skills, covering concepts like routing, security, troubleshooting, and infrastructure. This makes it ideal for entry-level IT professionals seeking careers in networking or cybersecurity. I am currently using a website called TestOut to study for the test. To remember the information presented in this course, I have taken to using a notebook and writing bullet points for everything. So far, a couple of things I have learned a lot about are the different types of cables used in networking, network topologies, and switches.
